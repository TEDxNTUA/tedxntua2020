What is this event?

TEDx is a program that consists of local, self-organized events that bring people together. It was created by TED and is under the general guidance of the TED Conference.

TEDxNTUA is a conference that features talks on the topics of popular science, art, social issues and stories that can inspire. It started in 2015 and is since run by NTUA volunteer students.
