How to get to The HUB Events?

Public transport

The venue is 7 minutes away on foot from the nearest metro station, Kerameikos (M3 line).

You can also get there by bus or trolley:

  • Bus lines 049, 815, 838, 914, Β18, Γ18 (Kampa stop)
  • Trolley line 21 (Kampa stop)

Use the Best Route tool from the OASA website to get a detailed initerary.
